Principles

The landscape keeps changing. What we believe in doesn't.

P / 01
We do what's right for your company. That's our one true north — and we're passionate about it.
Every call, every introduction, every offer is judged against one thing: is this the right move for the company we're working with? If the role shouldn't exist yet, we'll say so. If the candidate isn't right, we'll pull them — even if they've cleared every loop. Doing the right thing compounds.
P / 02
Your first hires will make or break your company. We treat them that way.
The people you hire in your first ten seats write the code, set the culture, and define what "great" looks like for everyone after. We don't run reqs — we hire founding teams.
P / 03
Senior people, on senior work. No junior sourcers learning on your role.
Every person you'll work with has held an in-house role at a company you've heard of, with hundreds of hires behind them. We don't farm out execution.
P / 04
Embedded means embedded. We use your tools, your brand, your standards.
No agency-style middlemen. Candidates speak to "your" recruiter. Your ATS is the source of truth. Your culture sets the bar.
P / 05
No hand-holding required. No back-and-forth on briefs.
We learn your stack, your culture, and the specific profile of person who will thrive in your environment — then we take full ownership of finding and closing them.
P / 06
We hire for where you're going, not where you've been.
Companies and stacks are changing faster than ever — flatter teams, people managing fleets of agents, judgement valued over process. We don't just match a CV to today's role; we find the people who'll still be the right hire as your company and its tools evolve.
P / 07
Hiring is a partnership on every front — you, us, and the candidate.
A great hire isn't a transaction; it's three parties getting it right at once. We work in lockstep with you on what the role really needs, and we treat every candidate as a partner too — honest about the company, the trade-offs, and the work — so the people who join do so with their eyes open and stay for the right reasons.

Great hiring is a partnership. Let's start one.
